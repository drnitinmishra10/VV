Women respond to the illusion of stability.

Every woman has a vulnerability. Find it. Is it money? Stress? Job frustrations? Low self-esteem?

“I have plans in motion. Once things go well, I’ll help you fix this place.”

“Once my business pays, we’ll upgrade your phone.”

Example 3: If her job stresses her:

“What business would you start? We’ll figure it out.”

If she starts doubting your words, solve a very small problem to restore belief. You don’t need to buy her anything big. Just fix something tiny and she’ll start believing again.

Once hope returns, access returns.
